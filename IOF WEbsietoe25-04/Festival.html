ABOUT

    The 36th edition of IO Festival is coming your way! The craziest annual student festival in the Netherlands is going to take place on June 1st 2018 at the faculty of Industrial Design Engineering at the TU Delft. More than 1600 students and employees will come and enjoy a wide variety of bands, DJs and other performances on three different stages for 9 hours long. The theme, Undiscovered Layers, will show the most undiscovered layers you have ever seen and absorb you into one of the best nights you will ever have! There will be three different ticket prices: A regular ticket costs €15,00. If you are a member of Study association i.d the tickets will be €11,50. There will be 100 early bird tickets available at the kick-off, those tickets will only cost you €10,00 Hurry to get your ticket, before you know it IO Festival Undiscovered Layers is going to be sold out!
